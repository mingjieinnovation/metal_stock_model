Read the English overview How to demo it

A Python research pipeline for Chalco and Zijin Mining. The project combines market prices, commodity futures, macro proxies, financial disclosures, production data and quality gates into reproducible valuation and monitoring reports.

What this project demonstrates

It is not a black-box trading bot. It is an evidence trail: every daily, weekly, monthly and quarterly output carries source status, cache usage, proxy flags and model boundaries.

Research boundary

When the data-quality gate fails, outputs are automatically downgraded to valuation anchor, observation or alert. Daily signals are treated as deviation alerts only.

Pipeline shape

01 — AcquireFetch equities, futures, macro data, filings and production disclosures.
02 — AuditRecord source, freshness, parse confidence, proxy use and missing fields.
03 — EvaluateBuild factor layers and valuation ranges for both companies.
04 — GuardrailQuality gates decide whether outputs are valuation, observation or alert only.
